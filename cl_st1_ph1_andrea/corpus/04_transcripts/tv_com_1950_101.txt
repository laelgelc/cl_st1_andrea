America's first commercial jet transport takes another step towards service on the airways of the world. A giant Boeing Stratoliner is wheeled out for its maiden flight. Fitted with noise suppressors on the mighty jets, it begins 12 months of rigorous flight testing to qualify for a Civil Aeronautics Administration certificate. Then a fleet of 150 already in production will go into service. Crowds and rain fail to mar the maiden flight of America's newest queen of the airways.

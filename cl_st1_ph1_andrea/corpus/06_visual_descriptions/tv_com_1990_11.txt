The commercial opens in a softly lit studio with a plain grey background. A young adult blonde woman with long, smooth, shoulder-length-to-chest-length hair leans forward with her forearms resting in front of her. She wears a black sleeveless top and a chunky silver watch. At first her hair swings across part of her face, then settles around her shoulders as she looks directly toward the viewer and speaks, calm and composed.

The scene shifts to a young adult man with dark brown, rounded, medium-length hair standing in a shiny metallic corridor, surrounded by tall reflective silver panels. He wears a fitted black short-sleeved shirt. He playfully raises his arms, first striking an exaggerated pose and then flexing both biceps with a broad grin, visually matching the idea of strength.

A different young adult man appears far away at the top of a long, pale, modern ramp between two white rails. He runs toward the viewer from a dark rectangular opening in a concrete-looking wall. As he comes closer, he is visible in a black suit with a light blue shirt and tie, his dark hair tousled by motion. He smiles and opens his arms wide, then pauses on the ramp with his arms stretched out.

Another young adult man appears in a warm, softly lit interior. He has straight, medium-length light brown hair combed back from his face and wears a pale green shirt. He leans forward with his hands together near the lower edge of the image, looking directly ahead with a small, confident smile.

The blonde woman returns in the grey studio, now closer. Her long hair lies sleekly over one shoulder and partly across her forehead. Her hands rest one over the other in front of her, the silver watch visible. She continues addressing the viewer, maintaining eye contact and a slight smile.

Back in the modern city setting, the suited young man stands amid tall glass-and-steel skyscrapers. He points straight toward the viewer with an extended finger, smiling. He then bends slightly forward, still playful, before reappearing on the long white ramp. In a close view he lifts both hands toward his hair, indicating it with his fingers, and then, from farther back on the ramp, he turns his body and points outward again as if making a choice.

A middle-aged man in a business suit appears in a more cramped, practical setting, holding a telephone receiver to his ear. He has light blond hair that is thinner on top. While speaking on the phone, he bends his head down and uses his free hand to smooth and pull his hair back over the crown of his head, revealing the scalp briefly before the hair is arranged more neatly.

The Pantene Pro-V products are then introduced visually. Two white bottles stand upright in rippling blue water, surrounded by bright, icy reflections. Their labels read “PANTENE PRO-V,” with small colored Pro-V symbols and product text near the bottom. The bottles gleam as the water and light shimmer across their curved surfaces, then the image washes into bright white.

A dark, backlit shower scene follows. A person’s bare shoulders and head are seen from behind under falling water. Both hands massage the scalp through wet hair as droplets sparkle against the dark background.

The commercial then uses a stylized hair-care demonstration. Golden, translucent capsule-like shapes float around enlarged brown hair strands. They drift between the strands, touch the shafts, and seem to attach or sink into them. The hair strands are shown at extreme magnification, with rough, scale-like edges visible at first. As the golden capsules move along the strands, the surfaces appear smoother and more uniform. The capsules continue traveling among many parallel strands, suggesting treatment moving through the hair from near the base toward the tip.

The imagery returns to people with healthy-looking hair. A young man with dark brown hair is first seen softly out of focus in a bright interior, resting his chin or hands together in front of him; as the focus clears, he smiles gently. Another young man with blond hair appears in close view, his hair neatly swept back with a glossy shine as he smiles and looks downward. Then a cheerful young red-haired boy sits on the shoulders of a smiling adult man. The boy, wearing a pale T-shirt, holds and ruffles the man’s hair with both hands while both laugh, framed by a bright, airy room with tall windows.

A larger product display appears: several white Pantene Pro-V bottles stand in shallow, rippling water. Two main bottles are centered in front, one tall and cylindrical and one shorter with a rounded top, with additional bottles blurred behind them. The “PANTENE PRO-V” name is clearly printed in black on the front labels, and small colored Pro-V icons mark the different products. Water droplets and bright reflections cover the packaging, giving the bottles a cool, clean, glossy look.

A close view of a blond young man follows against a warm amber background. He bends his head forward and then tosses his hair back, the hair sweeping in a bright arc. He runs a hand through the front, and the hair settles smoothly with a visible shine. White on-screen text appears across the upper left to upper center: “For hair so healthy it shines.”

The final image brings together the blonde woman from the opening and the light-brown-haired young man. They are close together in the soft grey studio lighting. He stands slightly behind and to her left, smiling warmly at her, while she faces forward with her long blonde hair smooth around her shoulders. She smiles toward the viewer as the commercial ends.

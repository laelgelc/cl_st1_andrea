A teenage girl with long brown hair hurries up a carpeted staircase in a bright home, carrying large glossy green shopping bags. She is dressed in a school-uniform-like outfit with a dark jacket, light shirt, skirt, and boots. At the bottom of the stairs, a woman with short brown hair in a white blouse watches her from the wooden hallway, looking up as the girl climbs past.

The girl reaches a room and sets a bright orange bottle of V8 Splash beside a black stereo system. The bottle has a yellow cap and a tropical label with the large “V8 Splash” logo. A hand presses a button on the stereo while the bottle stands prominently nearby.

Inside a cluttered, sunlit bedroom, the teenager begins trying on clothes. The room has tall windows with pale curtains, clothes and accessories scattered around, a yellow chair, an oval full-length mirror, and a dress form. She kicks and swirls a vivid orange skirt or coat around her legs, then poses in different outfits. The orange V8 Splash bottle appears on a table near the mirror, acting almost like another accessory in the room.

She lifts the bottle and drinks directly from it. In close-up, her eyes are relaxed as she tips the clear plastic bottle to her mouth; the orange drink fills most of the bottle, and the “V8 Splash” label is visible near her hand. She wears a bright, fashionably mixed outfit with blue and purple tones and a green scarf at her neck.

Other teenage girls appear in the bedroom, also dressed in bold, playful fashion combinations. One blonde girl in a large black floppy hat drinks from the same kind of orange V8 Splash bottle, her eyes closed as she sips. Another girl stands near the mirror holding a bottle, wearing a plaid vest over a light shirt and a short skirt, smiling and posing as if modeling. Another outfit change shows a girl in a shiny blue jacket and purple hat, adjusting purple tights near the bed and mirror.

The dressing-up continues with more exaggerated styles. One girl smiles beside a clear round fishbowl with small orange fish inside; she wears a leopard-print jacket over a red top and patterned clothing. Later, the main girl appears in a long orange coat, drinking from the bottle while standing in front of the oval mirror, her reflection showing the same orange coat. In another mirror view, she wears a lime-green dress with a blue trim and a darker jacket, while a V8 Splash bottle stands on a nearby surface. She smiles at herself and seems pleased with the look.

A close-up returns to the main girl’s face. She tilts her head and gives a sly, satisfied smile, her long hair framing her face and a green scarf still visible at her neck. Behind her, an orange drink bottle sits out of focus. Then the V8 Splash bottle is shown large in the foreground on a table, with another girl reflected in the mirror behind it. The girl in the reflection wears a black dress, a tiara-like headpiece, and a fluffy pink feather boa, posing theatrically.

The mother appears in close-up, now holding a clear glass of orange drink. She wears a white blouse and smiles knowingly as she takes a sip, looking toward the camera with a calm, amused expression.

The teenager comes back down the staircase in a dramatically coordinated outfit. She wears a black jacket over a white shirt with a red tie, red tights, and an extremely long, bright orange wig or hairpiece that falls on both sides of her face down past her chest. In one hand she carries the orange V8 Splash bottle. She pauses on the stairs and gives a playful, self-assured look, the orange drink visually matching her orange hair and red-orange clothing accents.

The commercial ends with a product still life: a large V8 Splash Tropical Blend bottle lying among tropical fruits, including sliced limes, kiwi, pineapple, and other fruit. The label reads “New V8 Splash Tropical Blend.” Across the bottom, large white handwritten-style text says, “KEEP THE PEACE. DON’T TELL THE KIDS.”

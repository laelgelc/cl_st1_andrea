A large white sign fills the back wall of a dark, stage-like room. In bold black lettering it reads “2 PIZZAS” above a huge price, “$5.99,” with the dollar sign and numerals dominating the center. In front of the sign, low in the foreground, stands a small puppet-like or costumed figure with a dark head and a yellow, pizza-patterned body, almost silhouetted against the bright sign. The room around the sign is dim, with reddish steps or flooring at the bottom. Small white fine print appears along the bottom: “Limited time offer in participating stores. Pricing may vary.”

The scene shifts to a male-presenting adult seen from the shoulders up in a dark, theatrical space. He has a mustache, short neatly combed hair, and large dark rectangular glasses. He wears a light-colored shirt and faces forward with a serious, slightly stunned expression. A strong spotlight shines from above and behind him, creating a bright cone of light in the smoky darkness. As he remains mostly still, the top of his head begins to change strangely: his hair and scalp appear to lift or split, and thin, branch-like tufts of hair spring upward and outward. The hair stretches dramatically to one side like wild, windblown tendrils, while his face stays fixed and blank beneath the glasses.

His appearance then changes into an exaggerated Elvis-like look. He now has a large black pompadour hairstyle with heavy sideburns, oversized pink-tinted sunglasses, a mustache, and a white high-collared jacket decorated with sparkly studs and large red-orange circular patches. Gold chains hang at his chest. He faces forward under the same moody spotlight, expression calm and impassive, as if the transformation has simply happened to him.

The setting widens into a long, sparse room with dark green walls and a reddish floor. A simple chair sits off to the left. At the back is a doorway or wall panel with a round white light, and a bright beam from the right cuts through haze across the room. A lone male-presenting figure stands near the light, small within the empty space. He moves into the spotlight wearing a light patterned short-sleeve shirt and dark trousers. He leans backward with one hand raised near his head, his posture suggesting dizziness or disbelief. Soon he is moving more actively, stepping or lurching forward with one leg lifted and arms bent, as though breaking into a confused run or dance across the lit patch on the floor.

The commercial returns to the large white offer sign: “2 PIZZAS” over “$5.99.” In front of it is the Little Caesars Roman mascot, a small cartoonish figure in a toga, holding up pizza. Across the bottom, the Little Caesars logo and name appear with the slogan “Pizza! Pizza!” in large white lettering. Beneath that, smaller text reads “$8.99 in Alaska & Hawaii.”

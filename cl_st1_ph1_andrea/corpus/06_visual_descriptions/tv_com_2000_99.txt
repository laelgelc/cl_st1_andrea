The commercial opens in black and white in a sparse, staged room with a wooden plank wall and a simple couch. A young adult man with short dark hair, light stubble, a black long-sleeved top, jeans, and large over-ear headphones sits hunched forward on the couch, holding the headphones with both hands. He smiles awkwardly and keeps adjusting the headphones while flakes continue to shower down over him.

The blonde woman now holds a black handheld device with a cord, laughing broadly and leaning back as if she has been controlling the flake “blast.” In a wider view, she reclines across the couch, smiling, while the man sits at the other end, pulling at his headphones and looking toward her, still dusted with flakes.

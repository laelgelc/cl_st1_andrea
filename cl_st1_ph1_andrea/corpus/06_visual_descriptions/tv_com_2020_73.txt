The commercial uses a flat, brightly colored animation style. It opens on a lime-green sky with simple rounded white clouds drifting across it. In the center is the Straight Talk Wireless logo: the words “Straight” and “Talk” stacked inside white and black speech-bubble shapes, with “wireless” beneath in black.

The logo moves out of view as the same green sky remains. A stylized animated man appears in profile from the shoulders up. He has medium-brown skin, thick black hair swept up at the front, and a full black beard. He wears a light blue jacket over a pale yellow shirt, and a white earbud sits in his ear. Small black musical-note symbols float behind him, visually suggesting that he is listening to something and tuning out the world around him.

The focus drops to a line of oversized green dollar bills laid out like stepping stones across the screen. A person in olive-green pants and white shoes steps from bill to bill. As more of the body is shown, the person has a black bob haircut, a purple jacket over a white shirt, and a relaxed, dance-like posture. One hand is raised near the chest as if holding or checking a small object, possibly a phone, while they balance on the money path.

Another animated figure then bursts across the money path in a more energetic pose. This person wears an orange shirt, olive pants, a red cap, red sneakers, and white earbud cords hanging down. Their arms and legs are spread wide as if running, dancing, or leaping. In the background, a smaller figure in a white shirt and olive pants flips upside down on a narrow strip of money, while a small green shopping-bag-like object appears near the action. The scene keeps the same lime-green sky and white cloud shapes, with the large dollar bills forming the ground.

The ad then switches to a product display. A black smartphone floats upright in the center against the green sky. On its screen is a colorful equalizer-style graphic made of vertical bars in red, orange, yellow, green, blue, and purple. To the upper left, a black speech bubble displays “$199” in large white text. To the lower right, black text reads “SAMSUNG Galaxy A51.” The phone tilts and rotates slightly, briefly turning sideways while the colorful bars remain visible on its screen.

Next, the plan offer appears as large speech-bubble graphics. A white bubble on the left reads “$45/mo” with “NO CONTRACT” underneath. A larger black bubble on the right reads “UNLIMITED” in bold white letters, with “TALK, TEXT AND DATA” below and smaller text reading “25GB OF HIGH-SPEED DATA, THEN 2G*.” Tiny fine print appears along the bottom, including a note to see terms and conditions at StraightTalk.com.

A red silhouette map of the United States then fills the center-right of the screen. Inside it, white capital letters read “LARGEST, MOST DEPENDABLE NETWORKS*.” The green sky and white clouds continue in the background. More fine print stretches along the bottom in very small black text, giving details about actual availability, coverage, speed, and comparison claims.

The red map disappears, replaced by bold black text across the center: “TUNE OUT THE NONSENSE.” The same small legal text remains along the bottom while clouds sit around the words.

The bearded man from earlier returns, now shown full body on a long green dollar-bill road that narrows into the distance. He wears the light blue jacket, pale yellow shirt, olive pants cuffed at the ankles, white socks, and brown shoes. He stands with a loose, rhythmic posture, gesturing with both hands as if dancing or pointing along with what he is listening to. The green money path stretches beneath him, and the white clouds sit low on the horizon behind him.

The commercial ends on the Straight Talk Wireless logo again, centered over the lime-green sky with white clouds. In the lower right, small white text reads “Available at Walmart” with the Walmart spark symbol. Finally, bold black text appears to the right of the logo: “NO CONTRACT NO COMPROMISE,” while the Straight Talk Wireless logo remains on the left.

A wide desert landscape appears under a bright blue sky with scattered white clouds. Red rock mesas rise in the distance, and dry shrubs dot the sandy ground. A woman-presenting cowhand rides a white horse across the open desert. She wears a broad brown cowboy hat, a cream western shirt, brown leather gear with fringe, and a bright yellow neckerchief. An acoustic guitar rests across her lap as she sits upright in the saddle. Nearby, a black crow stands on the sand. The crow becomes the focus, tilting its head and opening its beak before scratching at the dusty ground. Its claws scrape around a partly buried prescription bottle; only part of the white label is visible, including the word “Price,” suggesting the bottle represents prescription prices.

The cowhand is then seen more closely on her horse, still holding the guitar, her long reddish-brown hair falling around her face beneath the hat. She looks calm and confident against the sky. A small prairie dog wearing a matching yellow bandana pops up onto her shoulder. It perches beside her neck, turns its head, and repeatedly opens its mouth as if responding to her. The prairie dog’s tan fur, round dark eyes, and tiny paws make it stand out against her cream shirt and yellow scarf.

The setting shifts abruptly from the desert to a bright, modern pharmacy. Rows of shelves filled with white and colored medicine bottles stretch across the room. Through a large opening at the back left, the desert and the white horse are visible, as if the cowhand has ridden straight in from outside. A middle-aged man in a dark cap and jacket stands in the aisle examining a product. Pharmacy staff work behind the counter in the background. The cowhand on the horse moves into the store, creating a humorous contrast with the clinical pharmacy setting. Small fine print appears near the bottom in some pharmacy scenes reading, “GoodRx is NOT insurance.”

Behind the shelves, an older man with glasses, a balding head, and a gray mustache looks up with a puzzled, slightly wary expression as the cowhand passes by in the foreground. Medicine bottles and product boxes frame him on both sides. The cowhand then holds up a smartphone in one hand. The phone has a bright yellow screen with the black GoodRx name visible, matching the yellow scarf she wears. She smiles as she presents the phone, making the app the center of attention.

At the pharmacy counter, a woman customer with long gray-blonde hair, a mint-green shirt, jeans, and a large brown shoulder bag leans forward toward the register. Behind the counter, pharmacy employees in white coats and blue scrubs watch or assist. The cowhand sits on the white horse to the left of the customer, waiting in line area marked by black queue posts. The customer appears to be checking out, while the horse stands calmly beside her in the store.

The cowhand, still seated high on the horse, looks down toward the counter and customer. A receipt printer on the counter begins feeding out an absurdly long strip of white paper, stretching far down from the machine. The prairie dog appears again on the cowhand’s shoulder, now holding a small yellow phone or app-shaped card with “GoodRx” visible on it. The prairie dog raises it proudly beside its face. The customer and staff look toward the transaction area as the printer continues, then the printer’s green indicator lights come on and the long paper retracts or stops, leaving only a short printed stub.

The customer smiles broadly and lifts a small receipt in celebration while holding her own phone in her other hand. The cowhand smiles down at her from the saddle, still holding the yellow GoodRx phone. The pharmacy shelves and staff remain in the background, and the white horse’s shoulder fills the left foreground.

The commercial ends on a solid bright yellow background. The prairie dog in its yellow bandana stands upright in the center behind the large black “GoodRx” logo, waving or lifting its little paws around the letters. Finally, the prairie dog disappears, leaving the black “GoodRx” wordmark centered on yellow. Beneath it, smaller text reads, “Save time and money on prescriptions,” and a rounded white button below says, “CHECK GOODRX.”

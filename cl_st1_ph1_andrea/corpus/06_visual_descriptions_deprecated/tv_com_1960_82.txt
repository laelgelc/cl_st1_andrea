The commercial opens in a wide, staged interior that resembles a luxurious terrace or salon. There is an orange-brown carpeted floor with curved steps or a sunken seating area in the foreground, cushions on the left, and a raised platform at the back. Tall curtains, plants, a balustrade, lattice panels, a flower arrangement, and a decorative chest or cabinet are visible. A woman in a flowing pale/gold dress dances on the raised area, holding the fabric out so it billows around her.

After the opening wide shot, the sampled frames move into a rapid series of close-ups of feet and shoes moving across the carpet. The camera stays low, near floor level. Visible footwear includes women’s pale or white high-heeled shoes, light blue shoes, pink-toned shoes, and men’s shiny black dress shoes with trouser legs. The feet step, tap, cross, and lift in quick dance-like motions. The orange carpet remains the dominant surface throughout these shots. Motion blur suggests fast movement.

Intercut with the dancing feet is a woman in a pale yellow/gold dress lying or reclining on the carpet near the steps. In one shot she is shown from the side, propped partly on one arm, with the fabric of her dress spread around her. In several close-ups, her hand rests on or moves across the carpet, emphasizing contact with the floor surface. Her face is briefly visible in one frame, looking downward toward the carpet.

The middle portion continues with many close-ups of shoes and legs crossing the frame. The framing is tight enough that the dancers’ full bodies are not visible. The shots alternate among polished black men’s shoes, light women’s heels, and brown shoes. The feet move in coordinated, rhythmic patterns across the same warm-toned carpeted floor. The reclining woman’s dress and hand remain visible in the background in some shots.

Near the end, the camera returns to a wider view of the set. The woman in the pale dress reclines on the carpeted foreground area, surrounded by cushions, while several dark-clad figures appear in the background on or near the raised platform behind the balustrade. The ornate room, plants, curtains, and decorative furniture are again visible.

The final frames cut back to close-ups: first of the woman’s hand resting on the carpet beside the folds of her dress, and then an extreme close-up of a woven edge or fabric label attached to a carpet or textile. The label has embroidered yellow lettering, but only part of the text is visible and it is not fully readable in the sampled frame.

In black and white, a woman is in a shower against a wall of square ceramic tiles. Only her wet head, bare shoulders, and upper chest are visible. She has short dark hair plastered to her forehead and cheeks. A strong shower spray pours diagonally from the upper right toward her, forming bright white streaks of water. At first she has her eyes closed and looks content, rubbing one shoulder with a washcloth or sponge while water runs over her face and body.

When she appears to be addressed from offscreen, she opens her eyes and becomes suddenly alert. She clutches the washcloth close to her chest with both hands, her eyes widening dramatically. She looks upward and to the side as if trying to locate the speaker, then relaxes into a broad smile. As she begins speaking, her expression changes from startled to enthusiastic; she smiles, laughs, and keeps bathing while the shower continues to spray steadily beside her.

She turns her head toward the water, rubbing her shoulders and chest with the washcloth. She leans into the stream, lets the spray hit her face, and grins with her eyes squeezed shut. At moments she lifts both hands, one holding the cloth, and gestures animatedly while talking. She seems to enjoy the abundance of hot water, repeatedly moving in and out of the spray, sometimes facing forward and sometimes turning in profile toward the showerhead.

Still in the same tiled shower, she becomes more playful and expressive. She raises her arms high, laughs with her mouth open, and beams directly ahead. Water glistens on her face, neck, and shoulders. Then she lowers her arms and suddenly ducks downward, disappearing almost completely out of view; for a few moments only the tiled wall and the diagonal shower spray remain visible, with just the top of her wet head briefly at the bottom edge.

She rises back into view from below, looking up toward the water, then turns with a startled, comic expression. She now has a long-handled bath brush or back scrubber. Holding it across her shoulder and chest, she looks over her shoulder with wide eyes, then resumes talking. Her face shifts into exaggerated grimaces and smiles as she uses the brush to scrub her back and shoulders. She reaches one hand into the shower stream, letting water splash against her fingers, then continues washing, alternating between facing the spray and turning her back toward it.

Near the end, she has her back mostly to the viewer and scrubs between her shoulders with the long-handled brush while the water pours over her right side. She then turns her head back over her shoulder, reacting as if newly self-conscious. Her expression changes from surprise to a slightly embarrassed smile, still wet and framed by the tiled wall and bright spray.

Dark vertical bars slide across the image, partially obscuring her in the shower. The picture changes to a simple graphic: large bold text reads “OIL HEAT,” with a thin roofline shape above the words. In the final clear view, small text underneath reads “NATIONAL OIL FUEL INSTITUTE.”

Seen through the front window of a barber shop, a slim young white man with short blond hair and a pale pink shirt pauses outside under a striped awning. Inside, an older balding barber in a light blue short-sleeved smock holds a large black portable stereo near the window. The young man moves toward the entrance, glancing in with a startled, wary look.

The balding barber stands smiling beside an empty red-and-white barber chair, holding a striped haircutting cape as if inviting the young man to sit. Other barbers in matching light blue smocks work in the background. The young blond man’s face fills the view; his eyes are wide open and fixed ahead, his mouth closed, as if he is suddenly uncertain about what he has walked into.

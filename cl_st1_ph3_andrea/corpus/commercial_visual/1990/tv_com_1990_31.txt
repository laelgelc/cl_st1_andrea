Red and orange rock formations rise on both sides, with a shadowed valley stretching between them and hazy mountains in the distance. Against an orange sunset sky, a lone silhouetted figure wearing a large feathered headdress moves along a ridge, holding one arm forward as if carrying or examining something.

The scene shifts to a bird gliding over pale orange desert rock, its dark body and shadow crossing the sunlit ground. A green lizard then appears on a rough dark rock, its long tail trailing behind it. It climbs and pauses, alert, with its head lifted against a soft, sandy-colored background. As the lizard remains perched, the desert landscape blends into towering red rock columns, where mountain bikers begin to appear on a sloped trail.

A rider crouches low over the handlebars, legs bent and tense, moving through the sunlit haze.

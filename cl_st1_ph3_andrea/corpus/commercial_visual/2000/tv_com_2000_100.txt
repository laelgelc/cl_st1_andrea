The commercial opens on a nearly blank, softly lit white background with a faint gray vignette around the edges. A turquoise blur briefly appears at the side, then a translucent teal capsule comes into view, floating in the empty space. The capsule is glossy and rounded, like a gel pill filled with liquid, and the white word “Advil” is printed across its side. As it glides and turns, its see-through shell catches highlights, and darker liquid-looking shapes shift inside it.

The capsule continues moving against the plain white background, sometimes appearing large and centered, sometimes smaller as if speeding across the open space. Its teal color stands out sharply against the pale surroundings, and the “Advil” lettering remains the main identifying mark when the capsule faces forward.

A tiny teal dot appears near the center of the otherwise blank screen. Below it, small gray legal text becomes visible, then large blue capital letters spelling “HEADACHES” rise across the lower half of the screen. Beneath that word is a smaller legal line reading, “Tylenol and Extra Strength Tylenol are trademarks of McNeil Consumer Brands Inc.” The teal dot hovers above the word, then the Advil capsule settles horizontally above “HEADACHES,” centered like a bar over the text.

The capsule then appears to open or split at the middle. A surge of blue-green liquid bursts downward and outward from the center, splashing over the word “HEADACHES.” The capsule halves remain visible on the left and right, with partial “Ad” and “vil” lettering on them, while the central splash grows into a forceful wave. The large word underneath is obscured and seems to be overwhelmed by the rush of liquid. The blue-green splash expands until it fills most of the screen, turning the image into a soft wash of teal.

The scene clears to a product display on the same clean white background. A dark teal box of Advil Liqui-Gels sits centered, with large yellow-green “Advil” lettering and “LIQUI-GELS” printed beneath it. A small image of teal gel capsules appears near the bottom of the box, along with a white label area identifying the Liqui-Gels product. In the upper left corner of the screen, black text reads “USE AS DIRECTED,” and in the upper right corner, the red “Wyeth” name appears.

Bold slogan text appears below the box: “LIQUID FAST” in large blue letters and “ADVIL STRONG” in darker, heavier letters beneath it. The final image keeps the Advil Liqui-Gels box centered above the slogan, while a small teal capsule streaks past on the right side, reinforcing the image of fast-moving liquid gel relief.

The images are in stark black and white. A large, formal boardroom appears, with a long glossy conference table stretching toward the viewer and rows of empty leather chairs along both sides. The walls are wood-paneled, the ceiling is dark with small recessed lights, and the atmosphere is cold and corporate. Three male-presenting executives in dark suits and ties sit far apart around the table, their reflections visible on the polished surface.

The view moves among the men. An older man with swept-back white hair sits in profile, while a thinner, balding middle-aged man in a suit sits farther down the table, looking forward with a controlled, serious expression. Another white-haired older executive is shown seated alone near the end of the table, facing across it as if speaking or listening during a business meeting.

Then the visual tone turns unnatural. The white-haired executive suddenly jerks and bends forward, and another suited man seems to lunge or emerge from his body, as if a second executive is being produced out of him. The first man leans back with his head tilted upward while the emerging man plants his hands on the table and pushes himself forward. The same disturbing pattern continues: the balding man’s face tightens, his head and shoulders blur and contort, and his body appears to split or transform into another suited businessman.

The boardroom begins filling with more male-presenting executives in dark suits. Some stand, some sit, and others are caught mid-lurch, leaning backward or forward as if more men are being generated from them. Their faces are mostly stern, strained, or blank. Around the conference table, the figures multiply quickly, crowding the once-empty room. Several men stand shoulder to shoulder near the walls; others turn their heads or stare across the room. One white-haired man steps forward through the crowd, while behind him another man appears to be forced backward by the arrival of yet another suited figure. The glossy table now reflects the dense cluster of bodies.

The room becomes packed with businessmen. A balding man stands in the center looking alert and tense, surrounded by many similar men in suits. In the background and along the sides, more executives keep appearing and shifting, creating the impression of unchecked growth inside the corporate space.

The scene changes to the outside of a glass-walled office or conference room. Behind the tall window panels, a large crowd of suited men presses close together, filling the room from side to side. Their hands and faces are visible through the glass. The glass then becomes covered with a speckled, cloudy pattern, obscuring the men behind it until they look like a pale, grainy mass.

From above, groups of dark-suited figures move across city pavement. Seen from high overhead, the men look small and numerous, with long shadows stretching beside them. They stream and cluster together in an urban street grid between tall buildings. The crowd becomes a dense, dark, rounded mass moving through an intersection, like a swarm.

That crowded mass is then visually overlaid inside a medical chest X-ray. The ribs and lungs fill the screen in high-contrast black and white, and the clustered crowd appears as a dark, spreading spot inside one lung, resembling a tumor or diseased area. The X-ray image becomes clearer and steadier, showing the rib cage, spine, and both lungs, with the abnormal cloudy patch on the viewer’s right side of the chest image. Across the lower center of the X-ray, black text appears and becomes readable: “As they continue to grow, we continue to die.” The final image holds on the X-ray with that message, visually connecting the multiplying corporate figures to disease in the lungs.
